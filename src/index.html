About Me

Hi, I'm Sarah. My journey to software development began in a Beirut newsroom. When I was a journalist, I realized I wanted to tell stories in new ways, but I didn't quite know how I'd get there. I wrote about food safety and Syrian refugees and hepatitis and everything in between, but I wanted to do more than just write and I knew that stories can be more than just written. My love of interactive news apps led me to Nashville Software School, where I plan to combine front-end development with data analysis.
